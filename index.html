Our Story

A tree the ancients called sacred

Across the Mediterranean, the olive tree has been a symbol of peace, light and longevity since antiquity. In Tunisia — one of the oldest olive-growing lands on earth — some of our trees have been giving fruit for generations beyond memory. We named the company for that reverence.

Holy Tree began as a single family grove. Today we work hand-in-hand with growers across the Tunisian Sahel, pressing each harvest at its peak and bottling it at the source so the oil reaches you exactly as the grove intended.

  • Rooted in place. Single-origin fruit, never blended with imported oils.
  • Pressed at peak. From branch to mill in under 24 hours to lock in freshness.
  • Honest by nature. Every batch is lab-tested and traceable to its grove.

From Grove to Bottle

Four steps, zero shortcuts

01

Hand harvest

Olives are picked by hand at the moment of perfect ripeness, never stripped or shaken to the ground.

02

Cold press

Within 24 hours the fruit is cold-pressed below 27°C, preserving polyphenols, aroma and a low acidity.

03

Lab tested

Each batch is analysed for purity and tasted by a sensory panel before it earns the Holy Tree label.

04

Bottled at source

Filled and sealed at the grove in dark glass to protect the oil from light — fully traceable to its origin.

FAQ

Good to know

Our oil is mechanically cold-pressed from a single harvest with no heat, chemicals or refining. It tests below 0.3% acidity — well inside the extra virgin grade — and is judged free of defects by a sensory panel.

Every bottle is single-origin Tunisian, pressed from groves in the Sahel region. We never blend with imported oils, and each batch carries a code you can trace back to its grove.

Keep it sealed in a cool, dark place away from heat and light. Unopened, it stays at its best for about 18 months from pressing; once opened, use within two to three months for peak flavour.

Yes. We supply restaurants, retailers and distributors with bulk and private-label options, including custom bottling. Reach out through the contact form and our trade team will follow up with pricing.

We currently ship to 32 countries across Europe, the Gulf, North America and Asia. If your country isn't listed at checkout, contact us — we can usually arrange freight for larger orders.

The main harvest runs from late October through December. We press within 24 hours of picking and date every bottle, so you always know how recent your oil is.
