01Our Story

A tree the ancients called sacred

Across the Mediterranean the olive tree has stood for peace, light and long life. In Tunisia — one of the oldest olive lands on earth — some of our trees have fruited for generations beyond memory. We named the company for that reverence.

Holy Tree began as a single family grove. Today we work alongside growers across the Tunisian Sahel, pressing each harvest at its peak and bottling at the source, so the oil reaches you exactly as the sun and soil intended.

  • Rooted in place. Single-origin fruit, never blended with imported oils.
  • Pressed at peak. Branch to mill in under 24 hours to lock in the sunlight.
  • Honest by nature. Every batch lab-tested and traceable to its grove.

03From Grove to Bottle

Four steps, zero shortcuts

1

Hand harvest

Olives picked by hand at perfect ripeness — never stripped or shaken to the ground.

2

Cold press

Pressed below 27°C within 24 hours, preserving polyphenols, aroma and low acidity.

3

Lab tested

Each batch analysed and tasted by a sensory panel before earning the Holy Tree label.

4

Bottled at source

Sealed in dark glass at the grove and traceable to its origin row of trees.
